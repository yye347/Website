Part Two: Our Solution

You're the solution

That's right, you are our solution. Barry Curtis Park needs your time, your help and your friends! By volunteering, you and many others can form a team that can overpower the woes that Barry Curtis Park is facing at this moment. Together, we can achieve things that one can only dream of, and we can improve the park for the greater good of the thousands that visit the park every year. As a team, we can help clean up Barry Curtis Park by picking up stray rubbish, looking out for litterers, and most importantly, ridding the park of all of pollutants.

We need your help

1) Spread the word

We can't do this alone, spread the word, tell your friends, your family and your co-workers! The more the better, the more the faster we can give Barry Curtis Park a much overdue makeover. What are you waiting for, copy and paste this website's link and send it on all your group chats!

2) Get together

Round up all your contacts and agree on a time to meet. Remember the time and fill out the volunteer form on this website with the same date and time in mind. Our team will be unstoppable, and the rubbish will fear us. One can only dream, together we can do.

3) Gift your time

We don't need money; we need your time! Look at your schedule and pick out the best time that suits you. Whether it be on the weekends, or your lunch break, time can achieve things that money alone can't. Barry Curtis Park will thank you.
